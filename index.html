Index

🐜 Ants (your colony)

  • 👑 Queen — lays eggs in the egg room. Protect her at all costs.
  • 🐜 Worker — the basic all-rounder.
  • ⚔️ Warrior — hunts enemies and trash-talks its kills.
  • 🛡️ Guard — 3 elite bodyguards; they only ever defend the queen.
  • ✚ Nursery — feeds eggs so they hatch faster.
  • ✕ Caretaker — keeps eggs tended so they stay colored.
  • 🌾 Forager — gathers food on the surface.
  • 🍗 Food-collector — gathers food and raids the hive for honey.
  • ⛏️ Builder — digs tunnels and builds walls.
  • 💗 Drone — mates with the queen to speed up egg-laying.
  • 🚫🐝 Bee-hater — the weakest ant; just rants about bees.
  • 🐝⚔️ Bee-warrior — only ever attacks bees.
  • 💵 Renting ant — spends 1 food to rent assassin bugs (each rental makes the next assassin arrive 0.5s sooner).
  • 👑 Vincant — the immortal special drone (only on your team, one at a time). Strong, super-fast, acid-splinter attacks, hunts bugs, feeds eggs, patrols, trash-talks, and visits Beena in the hive.

🐝 Bees (the hive)

  • 🐝 Bee — stinger guard that swarms intruders near the hive.
  • 🛡️🐝 Armored bee — tanky, heavily-armored hive guard.
  • 👑 Queen Beena — the hive's queen bee (and Beena!).

🪲 Bugs (wildlife)

  • 🐞 Ladybug — harmless wanderer.
  • 🦗 Grasshopper — skittish; flees from ants.
  • 🥢 Stick bug — slow, harmless twig-like wanderer.
  • 🪲 Beetle — dangerous and tanky; attacks your ants.
  • 🗡️ Assassin bug — hired killer that hunts the hive's bees; spawns every 15s (sooner if renting ants rent more).
